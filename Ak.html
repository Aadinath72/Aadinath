Shivaji I-(Shivaji Shahji Bhosale,Marathi pronunciation:19 February 1630-3 April 1680)was an indian ruler and a member of the bhosle dynasty.Shivaji maharaj carved out his own independent kingdom from the Sulthanate of Vijapur that formed the genesis of the Maratha Empire.In 1674,he was formally crowned the CHHATRAPATHI of his realm at Raigad Fort.
